About the program

The Teams Program at Zaha Cultural Center gives children and teens a safe space to practice arts, technology, and sports with professional mentors. Each team follows a structured learning path that encourages collaboration, leadership, and real-world performance opportunities.

  • Certified instructors with proven experience.
  • Groups divided by age and skill level for personalized guidance.
  • Seasonal showcases and competitions to celebrate achievements.
  • Inclusive atmosphere that nurtures confidence and teamwork.
